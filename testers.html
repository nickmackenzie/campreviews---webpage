Welcome to Ontario Parks Reviews!

Thank you for trying out this app. This is version 0.0.1 of the extension. Here's how it works:

  1. Install the extension on Google Chrome.
  2. Visit Ontario Parks Reservations.
  3. Click on any campsite in any Ontario park to view reviews.
  4. If you log in via the extension icon, you can also leave reviews.

What's Next?
