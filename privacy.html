Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 4, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how the Ontario Parks Reviews extension collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using the extension, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

What Data We Collect

  • Personal Information:
    • Account Data: If you log in to submit reviews, we collect your email address and user ID to authenticate and associate reviews with your account.
    • Submitted Reviews: Information you provide in reviews, including ratings, text, visit dates, and uploaded photos.
  • Usage Data: Interaction data with the review widget (e.g., campsite selections, review submissions).
  • Optional Data: Uploaded images for campsite reviews.

How We Use Your Data

  • To Provide Services: Display and manage campsite reviews within the extension.
  • To Improve Functionality: Analyze anonymized usage data to enhance the user experience.
  • To Facilitate Authentication: Ensure secure login functionality and prevent unauthorized access.

Data Sharing

Your data will never be sold or shared with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: Hosting services (e.g., PocketHost) to manage and store reviews.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law to disclose information.

Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission.
  • Secure storage of user authentication tokens.
  • Limited access to data based on user roles and permissions.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your submitted reviews.
  • Delete or edit your reviews.
  • Request deletion of your account data by contacting us at contact.campreviews@gmail.com.

Third-Party Services

The extension integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Ontario Parks Website: For campsite data and map integration.
  • PocketHost: For storing and retrieving review data.

Each service has its own privacy policy. Please review their policies for more information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through the extension.
